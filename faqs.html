Ingredients & nutrition

What are Egg Life wraps made of?
Real cage-free egg whites are the first and primary ingredient. The rest is short and recognizable: modified food starch, salt, and small amounts of seasonings depending on the flavor. No artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives.
How much protein is in one wrap?
Each Original or flavored wrap has 11g+ of protein, 3g of carbs, and just 70 calories. Pre-made wraps with fillings have 14–15g of protein and 170–180 calories.
Are they gluten-free?
Yes. All Egg Life wraps are certified gluten-free and produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility.
Are they keto-friendly?
Yes — with 3g of carbs and 0g of sugar, our Original and flavored wraps fit comfortably into most keto plans.
Are they dairy-free?
Our wraps themselves are dairy-free. Our pre-made wraps with cream cheese contain dairy — check the label.

Storage & cooking

How should I store them?
Refrigerate after opening and use within 7 days. Wraps can also be frozen for up to 3 months — thaw in the fridge or briefly warm in the microwave.
Do I have to cook them?
Nope! Our wraps are fully cooked and ready to eat. For the best texture, warm them for 10–15 seconds in a dry skillet or microwave.
Can I crisp them up?
Yes! They make incredible quesadillas, taquitos, and chips. A non-stick pan over medium heat for 30–45 seconds per side does the trick.
