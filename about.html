Our Story

A wrap with one less compromise

We started Egg Life Foods because we couldn’t find a wrap that fit our goals without giving something up. So we made one — out of egg whites, not flour.

Born in a Chicago kitchen in 2018, Egg Life is on a mission to reimagine the foods you love with the ingredients your body deserves.

Why egg whites

Egg whites are nature’s most complete protein — packed with all nine essential amino acids, naturally low in carbs, and incredibly versatile. By starting with egg whites instead of flour, we’ve created a wrap that delivers 11g+ of protein, 3g of carbs, and 70 calories per serving without the bloating, sluggishness, or sugar spikes you get from a typical tortilla.

What we believe

Real ingredients

Short, recognizable ingredient lists. No artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives. Ever.

Better for every body

High protein, low carb, gluten-free, keto-friendly, dairy-free, and certified by the American Heart Association.

Made for cooks, not chemists

Soft, foldable, freezer-friendly, and ready in seconds. Use them anywhere you’d use a tortilla.

Made responsibly

We source from farms with high welfare standards and continuously work to reduce our environmental footprint.

Community first

Our Kale Creator and Dietitian programs put real people at the center of how we grow.

Always evolving

From our original wrap to pre-made favorites, we keep innovating to make eating better easier.

By the numbers

50+ million wraps sold · 9,000+ stores nationwide · 11g+ protein per serving

And we’re just getting started.
